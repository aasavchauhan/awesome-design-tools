## 🆓 Free & Open-Source Quick Guide

> Budget-friendly options are clearly labeled in the **Pricing** column across every section.

**Pricing labels**
- **Free** — no paid tier
- **Free / Paid** — free tier available
- **Free / Self-hosted** — open-source or self-hosted option
- **Paid** — subscription or purchase required
- **One-time** — single license fee

- ✅ **Accessible** — available to designers globally
